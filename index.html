IMPACT OF EARTH LEAKAGE

Electrical Shock Hazard

Earth leakage can lead to electrical shock, which is potentially fatal, especially in environments where individuals may come into contact with exposed conductive parts. This is particularly dangerous in residential, commercial, and industrial settings where people interact with electrical appliances and installations.

Fire Risk

Persistent earth leakage can generate heat, leading to the ignition of nearby flammable materials. This can cause electrical fires, particularly in older installations where insulation may be degraded.

Damage to Electrical Equipment

Earth leakage can cause damage to sensitive electrical equipment, leading to malfunctions or permanent damage. This can result in costly repairs or replacements.

Energy Loss

Leakage currents contribute to energy losses, which can be significant over time. This not only increases electricity costs but also reduces the overall efficiency of the electrical system.
